Our Direction

To lead India's transition to a sustainable energy future by becoming the nation's largest Bio-CNG infrastructure company, setting global standards for clean fuel production from agricultural waste.

To accelerate India's clean energy adoption by designing, building, and operating world-class Bio-CNG plants that convert agricultural residue into sustainable fuel, creating rural employment while reducing carbon emissions.
