Our Technology

Introduction to CSTR Technology

The Continuous Stirred Tank Reactor (CSTR) is the heart of our Bio-CNG production process. This advanced anaerobic digestion technology ensures consistent, high-quality biogas production from diverse organic feedstocks.

Reactor Type CSTR
Temperature 35-55°C
Retention Time 20-30 Days
Gas Yield High Efficiency
The Process

How CSTR Transforms Biomass into Energy

01 Feedstock Reception

Agricultural residue, cattle dung, and Napier Grass are received, weighed, and quality-checked at our facility.

02 Pre-Treatment

Feedstock is shredded, mixed with water, and homogenized to create optimal conditions for digestion.

03 Anaerobic Digestion

In our CSTR reactors, microorganisms break down organic matter at controlled temperatures to produce raw biogas.

04 Purification & Compression

Biogas is purified to remove CO2, H2S, and moisture, then compressed to produce 97%+ pure Bio-CNG.
